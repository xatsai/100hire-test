"Belief" is the most underrated skill when it comes to entrepreneurial success.

You have to actually believe you can do it.  

If there's some hidden thoughts resembling "success is hard for me" or "entrepreneurship is for others, not me", you'll subconsciously be searching for evidence of these handicaps.

At the same time, you'll be missing the opportunities and wins, even when they're staring you in the face.

Thinking back to my own journey and beliefs about success...

Admittedly, when I first started, I actually thought that making money on the interwebs was fake bs taught by gurus. And that real entrepreneurship is for Silicon Valley founders with MBAs.

Then I met people actually doing it. Actually making a living from online businesses. Thank you, Chiang Mai, for that.

✅ Belief unlocked

Then I made my first $100.

✅ Belief+

Then I got a website to profitability.

✅ Belief++

Then I built an agency.

✅ Belief+++

Then I built a conference, a course, and 3 more agencies.  

Where am I going with this...

Find evidence that you can do it. It's there. You're just overlooking it.

Where did you succeed before, no matter how small?

Celebrate your wins and integrate them. Make them a part of you.

Sooner or later, you'll be so used to winning that you've no doubt you can accomplish what you want.

Eventually...

Belief isn't a concept anymore. It's a knowing.
