AI search just hit a number that should make every SEO pay attention.

According to a new study by Graphite. io, AI tools now generate 45 billion monthly sessions worldwide.

That's 56% of total search engine volume globally. 34% in the U.S.

The kicker? 83% of that AI usage happens inside mobile apps, not browsers.

That means most estimates of AI search volume have been underestimating it by 4-5x.

Here's what the data actually shows:

- ChatGPT dominates at 89% of global AI sessions
- Gemini surged from 5.4% to 18.2% market share, a 237% jump year-over-year
- Claude grew 13x, driven by technical users

37% of consumers now start searches directly in AI chatbots instead of Google

AI isn't replacing search. It's expanding it.

Total usage across search engines AND AI assistants has grown 26% globally since 2023.

The pie is getting bigger. The question is whether you're visible in the new slices.

If you're only tracking Google rankings, you're measuring half the game.
