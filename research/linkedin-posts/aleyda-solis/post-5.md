🤔 Google has rebranded Looker Studio to ... Data Studio (again)! 

"Users need a single place to curate and analyze their data from the many different sources that impact their business each day... We are sharing the next step in our mission to solve this challenge and reintroducing a beloved and familiar name, Data Studio (formerly Looker Studio).”

So, welcome Data Studio, formerly Looker Studio, formerly Data Studio 🙌 😅

There's also a new Data Studio Pro: "designed for scaling teams and organizations that need more agility and control, including AI features and deep integration with Google Cloud for enterprise-grade security... Pro licenses can be purchased directly from the Google Cloud console or the Google Workspace Admin Console."
