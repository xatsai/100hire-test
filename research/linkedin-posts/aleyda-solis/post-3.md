🚨 After analyzing some of the ecommerce winners and losers from the Google March Core Update, the clearest pattern is that it redistributed visibility unevenly across query sets, with no single obvious explanation behind all shifts and a few late swings visible in the visibility trends. Having said that:

* When sites lost visibility, it often shifted not only to other retailers, but also to platforms, reference sites, and major brands. 
* Winners often had either stronger product specificity or higher non-transactional value. 
* Mid-tail commercial queries and queries with both commercial and informational overlap were more exposed to shifts. 
* AI Overview presence was relatively limited in the losing keyword sets.
