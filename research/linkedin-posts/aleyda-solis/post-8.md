🚨 Brand Bias in Prompts: An Experiment by Dr. Pete Meyers: How often did each type of prompt result in the presence of a brand in the output?

* Every prompt (100%) that included a brand returned one or more brand mentions in the output. For non-brand, this dropped by almost half (53%), with soft-brand prompts somewhere in the middle.
* On average, each brand prompt generated 14.5 brand mentions in the output, which dropped to 1.68 each for the soft-brand group and 0.79 each (<1) for the non-brand group
* The way we phrase our questions or even the topics that drive those questions can dramatically impact how an LLM surfaces brands.
