We had almost $500K in monthly recurring revenue sitting in our dead deals pipeline.

Prospects that told us the timing was off. The price was too high. Follow up in six months. And then just got stuck there and never moved.

We knew the money was in there. We just could never get to it. 

Every time we tried to do a re-engagement push it turned into a three day project for our sales admin. 

Pull the deals manually, dig through Gmail to find where each conversation left off, build a spreadsheet, write the emails, send them one by one. 

It was so much work that we kept starting it and never finishing it.

Today I did the whole thing in an hour.

Claude connected directly to our ActiveCampaign pipeline, pulled every dead deal, then went into Gmail and read the last email thread with each contact. 

It analyzed the sentiment, flagged the hard nos, excluded current and past clients, and built us a clean database of everyone worth reaching back out to.

I spent 20 minutes reviewing 309 contacts and marked the ones I wanted to pitch. Claude wrote a custom email for every single one, personalized based on what we originally pitched them and where the conversation ended.

75 emails. Loaded directly into Gmail. Scheduled to go out 4 to 5 per day so we don't flood our own calendar.

No code. No Cowork. Just Claude Chat connected to ActiveCampaign and Gmail via MCP.

The part that gets me is we always knew this money was sitting there. We just never had a realistic way to go get it without it becoming a massive project that nobody had time for.

Now it takes an hour.
