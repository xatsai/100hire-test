$300–$400 per click for “car accident lawyer”… worth it?

Maybe. 

If you can stomach $3–4k per signed case from Google.

But here’s the part nobody talks about: there’s a cheaper attention source sitting right in front of you.

Facebook.

Less competition. More work. You have to get on video.
But we’re seeing signed cases for nearly half the cost in competitive markets.

Google is point-of-search. Facebook is blue ocean.

In 2026, the firms willing to show their face win.

If you’re a smaller PI firm and Google CPCs make you sweat… it might be time to rethink the channel.
