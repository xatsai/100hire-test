I've been pitching "blended search" to clients for years. Last week was the first time I could actually show it to them in real time.

For 15 years the real insights lived in the overlap between SEO and PPC data. The problem was actually getting to them.

You'd need to pull Google Ads data, export Search Console, grab Ahrefs rankings, manually match everything in a spreadsheet with Vlookups, and even then what you ended up with was a table. 

No visualization. No ability to query it. Just rows and columns that most clients couldn't interpret anyway.

So the insight stayed in your head. You knew it was there. You just couldn't show it.

That changed for me this week.

I built a blended search report inside Claude that pulls all of that data into a single visual dashboard. Paid keywords mapped against organic positions. 

Keywords they're spending $50 a click on where a content push could eliminate that cost entirely. Keywords they rank page one for organically that they've never tested with a $500 paid budget.

Stuff that was always theoretically possible to find. Just never practical to actually surface.

I pulled this up on a sales call and the prospect stopped me mid sentence and said he'd never seen anything like it. Signed that day.

This isn't a custom data warehouse or a six month dev project. It's Claude with access to the right data sources and a prompt that knows what to look for.

We set it up as a skill. Every new client gets their own version in minutes.
