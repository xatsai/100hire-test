I've been running digital agencies for almost 15 years. Right now AI generates a lot of slop, and will continue to as it falls into the hands of folks who don't have the subject matter expertise to know what "good" looks like. 

Where AI is extremely helpful is workflow automation. 

The ability to query against Claude for performance on campaigns (i.e. "pull a report for this past week comparing leads from organic to paid channels. if there is a drop in performance, analyze any changes made to the campaigns. draft an update for the client for me to review").

That type of stuff is so beneficial to the client and it used to take us WEEKS to build something like that (just a few months ago).

The slop isn't going anywhere, but it's a good thing because then you can still create value.
