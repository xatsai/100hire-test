We used to have a VA overseas doing link prospecting by hand. 

It was slow, inconsistent, and we were paying for it every month whether the output was good or not.

I recorded a video showing how we replaced most of that process with Claude Cowork — and the results are kind of hard to believe until you see it happening in real time.

Here's what the workflow actually does:

— Prospects link opportunities in Google using our exact search operators (the same ones we've used for years) 
— Pulls DR and traffic metrics straight from Ahrefs into a spreadsheet 
— Runs every prospect through our link quality checklist automatically 
— Finds contact info via Apollo and builds the outreach email sequences

One person can now manage this for every SEO client we have. 

In 24 hours you could have a full prospecting and outreach database built across your entire book of business.

The thing I keep saying in the video is that Claude isn't magic — it works because we already had the SOPs. We just fed it the processes we'd already spent years building and it ran with them.

If you've been buying links from vendors because outreach felt like too much work, this is worth watching.
