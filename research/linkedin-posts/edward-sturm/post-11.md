A garage door company hired us for $18,000.

They made $1.7 million back in 11 months.

Optimized Google Business Profile. 

This is what helps them show in Google Maps and the local map pack.

Collected at least 10 legit reviews as fast as possible.

Used real photos, not cheesy stock images- please don't do use cheesy stock images.

Fixed hours, services, categories, description, everything properly.

Killed generic service pages on the website.

Built hyper specific money pages.

“Affordable Garage Door Repair in Houston - Same Day Service, No Hidden Fees.”

That's what you should be making pages like.

Fixed their internal links. The site had zero links between pages. Connected everything. The service pages, blogs, homepage. 

Focused on real solid backlinks.

Guest posts on real legit local blogs and industry sites.

If you want to see an easy way to use AI to get local backlinks, Google: "Edward Sturm The AI System to Find Relevant Journalists, Land Coverage, and Earn Ongoing High-Authority Backlinks."

Built relationships with other local businesses for link swaps.

If you're a plumber, you get a link from an electrician that you work with.

If you're an electrician, you get a link from a contractor you work with.

Stopped trying to be famous, focused on local only.

They wanted to rank for best garage door, top garage doors nationwide, pointless.

Doubled down on keywords like garage door repair Austin.
