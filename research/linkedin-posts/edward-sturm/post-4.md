These AI Overview things from Google… they reduce clicks to websites by 58%!

Websites that are targeting top of funnel keywords, these are informational keywords where people are trying to learn, these are what are getting destroyed the most by AI Overviews.

The AI answers a searcher's question, they move on.

To avoid this, go after bottom of funnel keywords.

These are searches where people know exactly what they're looking for. They are looking to use a product. They are looking to contact a service.

They just don't know the brand to go with.

They're putting in very specific high-intent searches.

Instead of targeting these people with articles, you target them with conversion-based SEO landing pages.

Before people start scrolling, right there, they get what they want.

It works for SaaS, it works for products, it works for services.

When you go after high-intent searches, even if you get one of these AI Overviews, it still recommends your brand.

People are looking to go to a website and use something.
