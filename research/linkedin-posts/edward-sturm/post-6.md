You know when you click the back button, but the same page just loads over and over again?

Google Search to penalize back button hijacking schemes. Finally.

Google is giving websites until June 15th to remove this malicious spam practice.

It's literally added right to Google's Malicious Practices section in Google Search Central.

For businesses that do this and don't remove it, they could have their entire websites blocked by Google.

The internet has been waiting for this, and it's finally come.
