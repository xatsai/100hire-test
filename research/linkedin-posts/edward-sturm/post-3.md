If a company ever pitches you scaling SEO content pages, you have to know this is what you should expect. You will get the most tremendous increase up.

This site had super sophisticated SEO content pages…

They increased from 200,000 clicks a month in two months to 9.5 million clicks a month.

Craziest spike up ever.

It was very easy for Google to identify a manipulation pattern.

This is what immediately happened… Huge downwards drop. Annihilation. Now they're at 47,000 clicks a month from Google. Way worse than when they first started.

Even last year, they were doing more sophisticated AI SEO than people are doing now in 2026. They still got hit.

There's a new SEO traffic pattern emerging. People are calling it Mount AI. AI content scaling starts, traffic spike, Mount AI peak, the website gets hit, visibility collapse, down and down and down and down and down it goes.

Don't fall for the hype.

Scaled AI SEO is unsustainable.
