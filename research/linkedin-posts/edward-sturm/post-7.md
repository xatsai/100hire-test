If you want your SEO to grow high like these New York City skyscrapers, do this.

Go to Google Search Console, this is Google's own tool for accessing their search index.

If you haven't connected your site to it already, do it here.

Then go to Performance > Search Results.

Now click on the blue AI button.

Use the prompt, “Find all purchase-intent keywords.”

Google Search Console will filter for keywords where people are looking to buy.

Now toggle on Average position.

Then scroll down to the Filter.

Filter for position is smaller than 7.

This means that you're seeing purchase-intent searches that your site is showing for beneath position 7, closer to the top.

Because your site is already showing for these searches, you just need a little extra nudge.

Click on a search term.

Now you will see the pages on your site showing for that search term.

Take the page with the lowest position.

Add your exact search term somewhere high on your page or add a new section for the search term.

Google will see that you made your page more relevant to the search term and show you higher for it, so you get more clicks.

Once you update your page, force Google to re-crawl it by submitting it to the top bar in Google Search Console.
