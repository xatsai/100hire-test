One of the biggest bottlenecks in SEO content production is revision friction.

You find an issue.
You leave a note.
Someone has to manually fix it.
Then you review it again.

Inside Rankability, you can now prompt the AI directly on any content asset and tell it what to change. It analyzes the content, makes the update in the editor, and shows you what changed.

So instead of flagging an issue like:
“The table in this post only shows six alternatives, but the list shows ten.”

...and then fixing it manually, you can let the AI handle the update.

It’s a small feature, but it points to a bigger shift:
less AI that only suggests, more AI that helps execute.

That’s where content workflows get much more powerful.
