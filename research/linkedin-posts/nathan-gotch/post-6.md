Building a solo AI-driven SEO agency sounds great...

Until you fire your team, absorb all their workload, and turn your business into a 12-hour-a-day job just to squeeze out more profit.

Not exactly glamorous.

AI and agents should empower your team to deliver better work. They shouldn’t turn your agency into a founder bottleneck that can’t scale.
