The biggest mistake an agency can make right now is treating AEO (answer engine optimization) as a separate discipline from SEO.

If you’re trying to sell them as two separate services, you’re going to have a hard time justifying it, because there’s so much overlap. Yes, AI search optimization has a few unique characteristics, but the overlap is massive.

In my opinion, you should be selling this as one unit. If you’re selling SEO, it should include the key elements of AEO and AI search optimization. Sell it as a “search everywhere” optimization package.

That’s the cleanest way to position it right now. It’s also the most valuable for the client. And yeah, you’ll probably need to charge more, but you should be charging more anyway, because doing SEO and AI search optimization is harder than ever.
